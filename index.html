Professional Grief Counselling

We provide skilled and experienced counselors who will walk with you before, during and after the funeral. Understanding that grief is a deeply personal journey, our compassionate counselors offer support and guidance to help individuals and families navigate through their loss and begin the healing process.

Professional Memorial Photography

We have the best team in the industry who ensure that the family gets quality livestream and photography that captures moments and keeps memories alive. Uniquely Crafted Caskets

We provide uniquely crafted caskets to meet family needs, bearing in mind this is the most important item at the funeral. Our selection includes a range of high-quality caskets designed to honor your loved one with dignity and respect while meeting your budget requirements.

Memory

Graveside Decoration Services

We provide graveside decoration services such as lowering gear, canopy as well as green carpets which are expertly laid out. Our professional team ensures that the final resting place is prepared with dignity and respect, creating a peaceful and beautiful setting for the burial service.

Coordination

Church Service Coordination

We liaise with the presiding clergy on the program, hymns, tributes, decoration and all other activities to be done during the service to ensure a seamless service. Our experienced coordinators work closely with church leaders to organize meaningful and dignified funeral services that honor your loved one's faith and traditions.

Media

Obituary & Media Services

Together with our partners and in consultation with the family, we process the placement of obituaries in the dailies, on local radio and TV stations. Our experienced team helps families craft meaningful obituaries that honor their loved one's life and legacy while ensuring wide distribution through appropriate media channels.

Professional Funeral Home Services

We ensure that loved ones are preserved with utmost respect and dignity at our partner funeral homes across Kenya. Our coordination services guarantee that your loved one receives professional care and preparation in facilities that meet the highest standards of quality and respect for the deceased.

COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

We're here to help answer your questions and provide clarity during this difficult time.

What does a funeral planner do?

We help organize every aspect of the funeral or memorial, from venue to ceremony details, easing the burden on your family.

Can I plan my own funeral in advance?

Yes, we offer pre-planning services so your wishes are clearly outlined and your loved ones aren't left making tough decisions.

Do you work with funeral homes and religious leaders?

Absolutely. We coordinate closely with funeral homes, clergy, and cultural representatives to ensure the service meets your values and beliefs.

How far in advance should we contact you?

You can reach out anytime for urgent needs or future planning. We're available 24/7 to support you and your family.

Are your services customizable to different cultures or beliefs?

Yes, we tailor each event to reflect your cultural traditions, spiritual practices, and personal preferences for a meaningful, respectful farewell.
